Instructions: Add your teams then name and give odds. Odds work like ping pong balls in a lottery.

So if Team 1 has 50, Team 2 has 25 and Team 3 has 25. Team 1 = 50% odds.

Numbers don't have to add up to 100, it's whatever your total sample size is.

For half percents just go up by a factor of 10. 17.5% = 175 with a populatio of 1000
